About

My name is Aaron Kahn I'm the sole proprietor of FlyIt4U. I was born and raised in San Francisco. I attended Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University in Arizona and graduated in 1996, after which I began a professional pilot career.

I started FlyIt4U in 2005 because I was looking for a new pilot challenge that would supplement my then non-flying business. I continue to do it to this day because I enjoy the challenges, I like to travel, and it's satisfying to get the job done safely. Even after a decade of delivery flights I still get excited when planning a new trip!

Professional Pilot experience: - Scheduled passenger airline captain Beech 1900 [US Airways Express] - Cargo airline captain single-pilot Beech 99 and Piper Chieftain - Corporate pilot Hawker 800 [Robert Mondavi Winery], Cessna 182 [Sandridge Investments] - Charter pilot Cessna Citation, Piper Saratoga My overall pilot statistics: 24 years as pilot, 22 years as a flight instructor 6,200 total flight hours 40 different types logged 1,600 hours of turboprop/turbojet My ferry pilot statistics: 11 years 58,000 miles 44 deliveries 29 crossings of the Continental Divide 9 international ferry flights
